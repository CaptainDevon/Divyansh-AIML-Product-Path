The Valorant Prelude to Chaos Vandal is a premium weapon skin known for its dark, apocalyptic theme.
It was released by Riot Games as part of the Prelude to Chaos bundle.
The design features jagged demonic shapes, glowing purple energy, and a futuristic corrupted-metal look.
Its animations include unique firing sounds, visual effects, and dramatic finishing moves on enemies.
The skin is popular among players for its intimidating aesthetic and immersive visual style.
