The Radiant Entertainment System Phantom is another stylish weapon skin in Valorant inspired by retro arcade gaming.
It is part of the Radiant Entertainment System collection created by Riot Games.
The design resembles a vintage arcade console with bright colors and pixel-style visuals.
Players can choose from different variants that mimic classic arcade game themes.
Its playful animations and nostalgic aesthetic make it one of the most creative Phantom skins in the game.
